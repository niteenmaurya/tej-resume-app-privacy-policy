1. What we store

All core resume data you enter in the app (work history, education, skills, projects, summary) is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not collect or store your email address or phone number on our servers.

2. What happens when you upload a file

When you upload a PDF, TXT, or DOCX file and choose to use AI-assisted parsing, the app:

  • First extracts any email addresses and phone numbers and **removes/masks them locally**.
  • Then, the app may send the remaining content (education, job titles, descriptions, skills, dates, and any other text present) to a third‚Äëparty AI/ML service to convert it into structured resume fields ‚Äî **only if you agreed** to this in the consent dialog.
  • Any other sensitive identifiers (for example: national ID, passport number, bank details, full home address) that exist in your file **may also be sent** if they were not explicitly recognized and removed by the app. For safety, please remove or mask such sensitive items before uploading.

3. Why we remove email & phone

Email addresses and phone numbers are removed so your direct contact details are never exposed to third-party services. This keeps your contact information private and local to your device.

4. AI accuracy & storage

The AI may return incomplete or incorrect information. You must review and confirm parsed details before saving. We try to use third-party services that follow privacy and security practices, and we request that they do not persist your data ‚Äî but absolute control over third‚Äëparty retention is outside our direct control.

5. Your control & consent

You are always asked for consent before any data is sent off-device. If you do not consent, no data is transmitted and everything stays local. You are responsible for removing any additional sensitive items from documents you upload.

6. Security

Keeping data local reduces exposure risk. We follow common security practices for any communication with third-party services, but remember: the safest option is not to upload documents containing extremely sensitive personal identifiers.

7. Data Deletion Request

As your data is stored locally on your device, deleting it is very simple.

  • **Local Data:** To delete your resume data stored on your phone, simply open the app and use the **'Delete Profile'** button, or **uninstall the application**.
  • **Third-Party Data:** Any data temporarily sent to the AI parsing service is processed **ephemerally** (not permanently stored) and is deleted immediately after use.
  • **Formal Request:** For any formal data deletion confirmation or query, you can contact us via the Contact Us tab.
