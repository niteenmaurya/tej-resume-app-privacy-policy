Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

1. Personal Data Handling

Tej Resume App does not directly collect or store any personal information such as your email address or phone number. All core resume data (education, experience, skills, projects, etc.) remains securely stored on your device only.

2. Third-Party AI Processing

When you choose to upload a PDF, TXT, or DOCX file, non-sensitive resume data may be temporarily sent to a third-party AI/ML service for processing. This is done only after you explicitly agree via the in-app consent dialog.

Important: Your email, phone number, and other sensitive personal information are never sent to AI or any server. They remain locally on your device at all times.

3. Data Security

Since your sensitive data never leaves your device, the risk of exposure is minimized. We ensure that any third-party service used for AI processing follows standard privacy and security practices.

4. Consent & Control

You have full control. You will always be prompted for consent before any resume data is sent for AI processing. You can choose not to upload files or use third-party processing, and your data will remain entirely on your device.
